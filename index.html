Experience Once In Your Life Time

Experience once in your lifetime the incredible history, culture, and natural beauty of Ethiopia. From the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela to the ancient city of Axum, there are countless historical sites to explore. Trek through the stunning Simien Mountains National Park, spot the rare Ethiopian wolf in Bale Mountains National Park, or marvel at the colorful landscapes of the Danakil Depression. Discover the unique cultures and traditions of the Omo Valley, try the delicious Ethiopian cuisine, and enjoy traditional music and dance performances. And don't forget to visit the National Museum of Ethiopia to learn about the country's ancient history. A trip to Ethiopia is sure to be an unforgettable experience filled with adventure, history, culture, and natural wonders.

Good Foods

Ethiopian cuisine is diverse and flavorful, with many delicious dishes to try. Some popular foods include injera, a type of sourdough flatbread made from teff flour, and wat, a spicy stew made with meat or vegetables, onions, garlic, ginger, and a variety of spices. Ethiopian cuisine also features a wide range of vegetarian and vegan dishes, such as lentils, split peas, and collard greens. Coffee is an integral part of Ethiopian culture and is often served as part of a traditional coffee ceremony. Overall, Ethiopian food is a must-try for any food lover looking for unique and flavorful dishes.

Travel Anywhere

Traveling anywhere in Ethiopia can be an incredible and diverse experience, given the country's vast landscapes, rich history, and vibrant cultures. Visitors can explore ancient historical sites like the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela, Axum's ancient ruins, and Harar Jugol's walled city. Nature lovers can trek the rugged and stunning Simien and Bale Mountains National Parks, spot wildlife like the Ethiopian wolf, and witness the colorful landscapes of the Danakil Depression. Ethiopian cuisine is also a delight, with its unique flavors and dishes like injera and wat. And, of course, visitors can enjoy the warmth and hospitality of Ethiopia's people, as well as coffee ceremonies and traditional music performances. Traveling anywhere in Ethiopia is sure to be filled with adventure, history, culture, and natural wonders.

Mountain Climbing

Mountain climbing in Ethiopia can be an exhilarating and rewarding experience for adventure seekers. The Simien Mountains National Park is a popular destination for trekking and hiking, and offers stunning views of the rugged mountain landscapes and diverse wildlife, including the endemic gelada baboon. Mount Bwahit is the second-highest peak in the park and can be climbed by experienced hikers, while the lower peaks in the park offer relatively accessible trekking opportunities. Mount Abuna Yosef is another popular mountain to climb in Ethiopia, with a challenging ascent but rewarding views from the summit. As with any mountaineering, it's important to be well-prepared and seek appropriate guidance and support.

Hot Air Balloon

Abyssinia Ballooning offers hot air balloon flights over the stunning landscapes of Ethiopia , including the Amhara region and overlooking the historical sites of the region.
